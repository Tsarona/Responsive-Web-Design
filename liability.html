Liabilities



Accidents do happen and other accidents may be unexpected. With beekeeping ones needs to expect eventualties when dealing with bees. That is where honeyQuotes™ provides assistance in the event of an accident.


Why you need this?

The benefits include:

  1. Beekeepers are required to place their beehives far away from towns and cities. This however does not mean bees can act on their natural instinct i.e a person can drive on a National or Regional road, where a colony of bees might sting him/her or there is a nearby cattle/horse farm, which cattle/horse odour can annoy bees and might sting the mentioned animals. We insure these kind of eventalities.
  2. Most of the times, beekeepers hosts master classes where members of the community can participate. An accident can occur anytime.


VERY IMPORTANT

It must be noted that the insurance does not cover employees injuries. It is the responsibilty of the employer to register their employees with COIDA.
